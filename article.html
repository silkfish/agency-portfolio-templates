Agency Portfolio Site Templates: What Actually Wins New Clients

Agency portfolio sites are some of the most over-designed sites on the internet. Most prioritize aesthetic novelty over the simple goal of winning new business.

After studying hundreds of agency portfolio sites and the conversion data behind them, the same patterns keep winning. Aesthetic innovation rarely correlates with new client wins.

What works on agency portfolio sites

  • Case studies front and center, not buried under "Work" or "Projects"
  • Real measurable results in the case studies (revenue, traffic, conversions)
  • Client logos visible above the fold
  • Clear service offerings, not vague "we do everything" copy
  • Process explanation that addresses prospect concerns
  • Easy contact path — usually a single form or email link

What does not work

Custom cursor effects, parallax scrolling, autoplay videos, and elaborate intro animations all measurably reduce conversions. Prospects came to evaluate work and contact the agency. Anything that delays those two goals hurts.
